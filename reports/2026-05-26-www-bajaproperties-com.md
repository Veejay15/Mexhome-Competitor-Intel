MexHome has `/area/los-cabos/la-paz/`, `/area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-condos-for-sale/`, `/area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-homes-for-sale/`, and `/blog/explore-la-paz-real-estate-from-downtown-to-beachfront-gems/`.
