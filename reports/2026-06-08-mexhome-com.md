# MexHome: Week of 2026-06-08

---

## 1. Executive Summary

- MexHome executed a significant URL restructuring this week, migrating 17 vacation rental and rental-type pages from standalone top-level paths (e.g., `/area/cabo-san-lucas-vacation-rentals/`) into their proper destination sub-hierarchies (e.g., `/area/los-cabos/cabo-san-lucas/cabo-san-lucas-vacation-rentals/`). This is a meaningful site architecture improvement with SEO implications.
- A press release announcing a strategic partnership with Naya Homes generated a burst of new backlinks from at least 6 referring domains this week. All links are nofollow except one dofollow link from nayahomes.com itself plus one from intently.co.
- Keyword rankings show broad positive momentum: 2,252 keywords moved up versus 1,979 down. The homepage gained position on several high-volume beachfront and Mexico City terms. The San Francisco Nayarit (San Pancho) area page swept to position 1 across multiple related queries.
- Three backlinks were lost this week, including one from a domain-authority-65 site (downbeach.com) that removed its link.

## 5. Recommended Actions for MexHome

1. **Capitalize on the Naya Homes partnership with dofollow link placement.** The press release generated links, but nearly all are nofollow syndicated articles. Work directly with Naya Homes to add dofollow editorial links from their existing high-traffic blog posts (e.g., their Puerto Vallarta condo post and real estate agencies post, which already link to MexHome). Request links to specific area pages, not just the homepage, to distribute authority to destination pages.

2. **Recover the downbeach.com dofollow link.** This was a domain-rank-65 site that removed its link deliberately ("link_removed" reason). Reach out to the editor at downbeach.com to understand why it was removed and negotiate reinstatement or a replacement editorial mention. This is the highest-quality link lost this week.

3. **Prioritize internal linking from newly restructured rental pages to their parent sale pages.** The 17 migration pages now sit correctly under their destination hierarchies (e.g., `/area/los-cabos/cabo-san-lucas/`). Ensure each new rental page includes contextual links to the corresponding for-sale pages (e.g., `/area/los-cabos/cabo-san-lucas/cabo-san-lucas-condos-for-sale/`) to pass authority bidirectionally and strengthen the destination cluster signal.

4. **Build out equivalent rental sub-pages for destinations that now have Los Cabos and Riviera Nayarit coverage but lack them elsewhere.** For example, MexHome has `/area/los-cabos/cabo-san-lucas/cabo-san-lucas-vacation-rentals/` but no equivalent rental pages under San Miguel de Allende, Mazatlan, Playa del Carmen, or Puerto Morelos, all of which have active for-sale sub-pages in the existing page list. Identify which of these destinations has rental search demand and build into the same nested structure established this week.

5. **Expand the San Pancho (San Francisco) area page to consolidate its position-1 rankings.** The `/area/riviera-nayarit-real-estate/san-francisco-mexico/` page moved to position 1 across four distinct queries this week. Strengthen this page with richer content (market stats, neighborhood sub-sections, local imagery) and add structured internal links from related Riviera Nayarit pages. MexHome already has `/area/san-pancho-vacation-rentals/` and `/area/san-pancho-vacation-rentals/san-pancho-apartments-for-rent/`, so cross-linking between the sale and rental sides of San Pancho is a quick win.

6. **Leverage the "mexico property for sale beachfront" position-1 gain with a dedicated beachfront landing page.** The homepage jumped from position 10 to 1 on both "mexico property for sale beachfront" and "beach property in mexico for sale" (combined volume: 640/month). MexHome does not currently have a standalone beachfront properties page. Building `/area/mexico-beachfront-properties/` or similar, internally linked from the homepage and major destination pages, could capture and hold this demand more durably than the homepage alone.
