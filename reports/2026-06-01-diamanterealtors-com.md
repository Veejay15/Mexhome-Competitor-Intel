## 5. Recommended Actions for MexHome

1. **Counter the Loreto and La Paz ranking surge directly.** Diamante jumped to position 3 for "loreto homes for sale" (480 searches/month) and "homes for sale in loreto mexico" and to position 3 for "lapaz real estate" (590 searches/month). MexHome has existing pages at `/area/los-cabos/loreto/`, `/area/los-cabos/la-paz/`, `/area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-condos-for-sale/`, and `/area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-homes-for-sale/`. Audit these pages for thin content, strengthen H1 and meta titles to match exact-match search intent, and add substantive local market copy, recent listings counts, and internal links from the blog (e.g., `/blog/explore-la-paz-real-estate-from-downtown-to-beachfront-gems/`) to push rankings above Diamante on these terms.

2. **Target the San Jose del Cabo condos cluster before Diamante consolidates it.** Diamante now holds position 2 for "condos for sale in san jose del cabo" (390 searches/month) across three keyword variants. MexHome has `/area/los-cabos/san-jose-del-cabo/san-jose-del-cabo-condos-for-sale/`. Expand that page with pricing context, community descriptions, and a structured FAQ targeting "condos for sale in san jose del cabo mexico" and "condo for sale san jose del cabo" to compete on the full variant set.

3. **Target the Los Barriles real estate keyword gap.** Diamante holds position 1 for "los barriles real estate" (390 searches/month). MexHome has `/area/los-cabos/east-cape/los-barriles/` but no dedicated property-type sub-pages for Los Barriles (no condos, homes, or land pages under that path visible in the existing pages list). Build out `/area/los-cabos/east-cape/los-barriles/los-barriles-homes-for-sale/` and equivalent pages to present a deeper content structure than Diamante's single aggregator page.

4. **Monitor and act on Diamante's sitemap purge.** Diamante removed nearly 500 pages this week, including dozens of neighborhood-level real estate pages (Bellavista, Chametla, El Centenario, La Posada, etc.) and several content pages. Some of these represent legitimate search queries that will now have a weaker or absent answer from Diamante. Where MexHome does not cover these Baja micro-neighborhoods (most fall outside MexHome's current footprint), assess whether any carry meaningful search volume worth targeting with new content.

5. **Disregard the new backlink spike as a quality signal.** The 60-plus new links Diamante received this week come from two low-authority comment-injection networks (streetwiseworld.com.ng at DR 29, deridez.communidee.net at DR 19). These are not a competitive threat from a link authority standpoint and do not warrant any reactive link-building response. Focus MexHome's link-building resources on outreach to legitimate real estate and travel publications rather than attempting to match this type of volume.

6. **Strengthen the La Ventana coverage before Diamante widens its lead.** Diamante holds position 1 for "la ventana baja real estate" and "la ventana mexico real estate" (both 170 searches/month). MexHome has `/area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-ventana/` but no property-type sub-pages under La Ventana in the existing pages list. Adding homes-for-sale and land-for-sale sub-pages under that path and adding internal links from blog posts about Baja living would create a deeper structure that could displace Diamante's single listing-aggregator page on these terms.
