## 3. Backlink Movements

SEMrush data was uploaded for this competitor this week (33 rows, caborealestate.com-backlinks.csv). The findings are as follows.

The backlink activity detected for Cabo Real Estate this week is almost entirely composed of low-quality, zero-authority spam. Every linking domain in the top rows carries a Page Authority Score of 0. The source titles are explicitly selling aged domains and backlinks ("Where to buy aged domains and backlinks"), and the linking pages contain 9,000 to 10,000 external links each, which is a clear signal of link-farm infrastructure. These links carry no SEO value and present no competitive threat.

The only link in the dataset with genuine potential relevance is from bajalivingco.com, pointing to an individual agent subdomain (alejandro.caborealestate.com). It is an image link, carries no anchor text with keyword value, and the source page has only 4 external links, suggesting it is a small but real site. Even this link targets an agent page rather than a core service or location page, limiting its impact.

Anchors used across the spam network reference competitor brand names including bajanext.com, remexico.com, bajagolfproperties.com, and realestateloreto.com, alongside caborealestate.com itself. This pattern suggests the same link-farm network is being used to build citations for multiple Baja real estate players simultaneously, or that the links were placed by a third party without Cabo Real Estate's involvement.

No meaningful editorial or niche-relevant backlinks were detected this week.

## 5. Recommended Actions for MexHome

The following recommendations are grounded in what the data shows about Cabo Real Estate's keyword profile and weaknesses, cross-referenced against MexHome's existing pages.

**1. Strengthen the La Paz homes-for-sale page.**
Cabo Real Estate just lost its position 23 ranking for "houses for sale in la paz mexico" (590 searches/month, transactional). MexHome already has /area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-homes-for-sale/. This page should be reviewed and strengthened now while Cabo Real Estate's equivalent subpage is unranked. Add fresh listing counts, updated copy, and internal links from the La Paz area hub (/area/los-cabos/la-paz/) and relevant blog posts.

**2. Improve the Cabo San Lucas homes-for-sale page to compete on lost Cabo San Lucas subpage traffic.**
Cabo Real Estate's /cabo-san-lucas subpage lost its ranking for "houses for sale in cabo san lucas mexico" (320 searches/month) this week. MexHome already has /area/los-cabos/cabo-san-lucas/cabo-san-lucas-homes-for-sale/. Review title tags, heading structure, and on-page content depth to ensure this page is positioned to capture the gap.

**3. Publish a fideicomiso explainer page or strengthen existing trust-related blog content.**
Cabo Real Estate holds position 3 for "fideicomisos" (260 searches/month) with a dedicated page at /the-fideicomiso-explained. MexHome has /blog/bank-trusts-in-mexico/ and /blog/fideicomiso-ownership-mexican-real-estate/. Check whether either post targets "fideicomisos" explicitly in its title tag and H1. If not, consolidate or optimize one of these posts around that keyword. A dedicated standalone page at /fideicomiso/ would also allow cleaner targeting and internal linking.

**4. Publish a Cabo real estate market report for 2026.**
Cabo Real Estate holds position 1 for "cabo real estate market" via a 2026 market report post. MexHome has blog content covering Los Cabos (/blog/los-cabos-real-estate-agents/, /blog/unlocking-opportunities-los-cabos-real-estate-investments/, /blog/maximize-your-profit-los-cabos-investments-in-real-estate-for-2025/) but no 2026 market report equivalent is visible in the existing page list. A dedicated 2026 Los Cabos / Cabo real estate market report post is a genuine content gap that can compete directly on this query and the broader "cabo real estate" informational cluster.

**5. Do not pursue backlink outreach to match Cabo Real Estate's current link activity.**
Their new link volume this week is entirely spam-network driven. There is no editorial link-building momentum to respond to. MexHome's backlink strategy should focus on quality over volume. The one notable exception is bajalivingco.com linking to a Cabo Real Estate agent page. If MexHome has agents active in the Baja market, reaching out to regional directory and lifestyle sites like this one for legitimate placement would be a better use of outreach resources.
