### Bucerias Content Gap Response

**1. Build a Bucerias buying guide page.**
Mexico Life Realty has `/bucerias-real-estate-buying-guide.php` and `/why-choose-bucerias.php`. MexHome has `/area/riviera-nayarit-real-estate/bucerias-mexico/` and sub-pages for condos, homes, and land, but no dedicated buying guide or persuasion page for Bucerias. Build `/area/riviera-nayarit-real-estate/bucerias-mexico/bucerias-buying-guide/` targeting "buying real estate in Bucerias" and "Bucerias Mexico real estate guide."

**2. Build a "Living in Bucerias" lifestyle guide page.**
Mexico Life Realty has `/living-in-bucerias-mexico.php`. MexHome has a blog post at `/blog/experience-everyday-living-in-bucerias/` and `/blog/bucerias/` but no dedicated area-level lifestyle page. Build `/area/riviera-nayarit-real-estate/bucerias-mexico/living-in-bucerias/` with deep lifestyle content (cost of living, neighborhoods, expat community, restaurants, transport to PV) to compete at the area page level rather than just the blog level.

**3. Build location comparison pages for Bucerias.**
Mexico Life Realty has five comparison pages (Bucerias vs. La Cruz, Bucerias vs. Puerto Vallarta, Bucerias vs. Nuevo Vallarta, Bucerias vs. Punta de Mita, Bucerias vs. Lo de Marcos). MexHome has none of these. These pages are high-intent for buyers in the consideration stage. Build at minimum `/blog/bucerias-vs-la-cruz/` and `/blog/bucerias-vs-nuevo-vallarta/` this week as blog posts, with the intent to eventually promote them to area-level pages. Target queries like "Bucerias vs La Cruz real estate" and "should I buy in Bucerias or Nuevo Vallarta."
