## 5. Recommended Actions for MexHome

### 1. Target the vacated "puerto vallarta real estate" cluster directly

Mexico Life Realty's primary page for "puerto vallarta real estate" (3,600 searches/month) has dropped off the rankings entirely. Their "/beachfront-condos-for-sale-in-puerto-vallarta.php" URL, which previously held positions 2 to 3 on multiple high-volume condo terms, has collapsed to positions 26 to 33. MexHome already has "/area/puerto-vallarta-real-estate/" and "/area/puerto-vallarta-real-estate/condos-for-sale/" covering this territory. Prioritize expanding and freshening those pages this week. Add recently sold data points, updated pricing context, neighborhood-level internal links, and explicit schema markup. The window to capture displaced traffic is now.

### 2. Strengthen the Puerto Vallarta homes-for-sale page to absorb spillover

"Puerto vallarta homes for sale" (2,900 searches/month) dropped from position 3 to 18 on Mexico Life Realty's site. MexHome has "/area/puerto-vallarta-real-estate/homes-for-sale/" for this. Audit this page for thin content, update the listing count, add a buyer-intent FAQ section, and build 3 to 5 internal links from relevant blog posts already published on MexHome (e.g., "/blog/buying-property-in-puerto-vallarta/" and "/blog/condos-for-sale-in-puerto-vallarta/"). Do not build a new page. Strengthen what exists.

### 3. Build a price-filtered listing experience targeting the under-$100k segment

Mexico Life Realty holds position 1 for "houses for sale in mexico under $100,000" and position 3 for "cheap homes/houses for sale in mexico" via a dedicated "/pool/under-100000/" URL. Cross-referencing MexHome's page list finds no equivalent price-filtered page. This is a genuine content gap. MexHome should build a filtered search results page or landing page targeting the under-$100,000 buyer segment. The keyword difficulty scores on these terms are in the 1 to 16 range. Combined search volume across the variants is approximately 960 searches/month with low competition. This is an achievable position-1 target.

### 4. Build a Mexico mortgage calculator page

Mexico Life Realty holds position 1 for "mexico mortgage calculator" (110 searches/month) with virtually no competition (KD: 2). MexHome's existing "/mortgage/" page does not appear to serve this exact query with a functional calculator. Cross-referencing the page list confirms no dedicated calculator page exists. Build a standalone "/mexico-mortgage-calculator/" page with an embedded, functional calculator. Difficulty is minimal and the tool creates engagement signals that support broader domain authority.

### 5. Pursue the Remitly linking pattern for MexHome

Mexico Life Realty earned a new link from Remitly's "How Much Does a House Cost in Mexico in 2026" blog post. MexHome has existing blog content on property costs (e.g., "/blog/buying-a-property-in-mexico-costs-and-taxes-explained/" and "/blog/price-of-ideal-homes-in-mexico/").
