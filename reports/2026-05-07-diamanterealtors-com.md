**Net movement:** No new or lost links are flagged in the top rows. The link velocity appears low and the profile carries significant spam risk.

Diamante's La Paz cluster is in freefall. "La paz mexico real estate" (1,300 monthly searches, KD 14) and "houses for sale in la paz mexico" (590 monthly searches, KD 15) are now open opportunities. MexHome already has /area/los-cabos/la-paz/ and the sub-pages /area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-condos-for-sale/, /area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-homes-for-sale/, and /area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-land-for-sale/. The priority action is to strengthen those existing pages: improve title tags and H1s to match exact high-volume query variants, add fresh market content and listing counts, and build internal links from relevant blog posts such as /blog/explore-la-paz-real-estate-from-downtown-to-beachfront-gems/ and /blog/what-to-do-in-la-paz/.

**2. Challenge Diamante on Los Cabos transactional terms.**
Terms like "los cabos homes for sale" (320 monthly searches, KD 12) and "cabo condos for sale" (720 monthly searches, KD 8) have seen Diamante drop from positions 1 through 4 to positions 10 through 15. MexHome has /area/los-cabos/los-cabos-homes-for-sale/ and /area/los-cabos/los-cabos-condos-for-sale/. Prioritize on-page optimization on both pages, focusing on content depth, structured data, and internal link equity from the existing Cabo blog content (e.g., /blog/buying-a-condo-in-cabo/, /blog/buying-real-estate-in-cabo/).

**3. Build editorial backlinks targeting fideicomiso and buyer-education content.**
The only credible editorial link in Diamante's visible backlink profile points to their fideicomiso explainer page. MexHome already has /blog/bank-trusts-in-mexico/ and /blog/fideicomiso-ownership-mexican-real-estate/. Pitch these pages to Mexico-focused real estate media (Mexico News Daily is the benchmark here) as reference sources. Diamante is earning coverage from this content type. MexHome should compete for the same placements.

**4. Evaluate La Ventana and Los Barriles as content expansion targets.**
Diamante owns dominant positions on La Ventana (positions 1 across all variants) and is now position 1 for Los Barriles. MexHome has /area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-ventana/ and /area/los-cabos/east-cape/los-barriles/ already in the sitemap. These pages should be audited for content depth. If they are thin, expanding them with neighborhood guides, current inventory context, and buyer intent content would put MexHome in position to compete in niches where Diamante is currently unchallenged.

**5. Do not pursue a link acquisition strategy resembling Diamante's.**
The Diamante backlink profile is a cautionary example. The majority of their 123 links come from wiki spam, unrelated low-authority blogs, and what appears to be a link-selling post explicitly naming their domain. This profile carries penalty risk and provides minimal ranking benefit. MexHome's link acquisition should stay focused on real estate directories, travel publishers, and editorial placements from credible Mexico-focused media.
