Cabo Real Estate just published content at /blog/Commercial-Real-Estate-in-Cabo. MexHome has /blog/buying-the-right-commercial-property/ but that page is not location-specific and will not rank for Cabo or Los Cabos commercial real estate intent queries. A new blog post targeting "commercial real estate in Cabo San Lucas" or "commercial real estate Los Cabos" would directly compete with the new Cabo Real Estate content while it is still fresh and has not accumulated authority.

Cabo Real Estate's /loreto-danzantebay page is generating meaningful traffic despite declining rankings, and the keyword cluster around "loreto homes for sale," "homes for sale loreto mexico," and "loreto real estate" (each with 480+ monthly searches) shows consistent demand. These pages should be reviewed for content depth and optimized to compete directly for the Loreto keyword cluster, particularly since Cabo Real Estate's rankings on these terms have weakened this week.

**3. Develop content targeting the Los Barriles keyword cluster.**

Cabo Real Estate's blog post on Los Barriles is gaining position on "los barriles real estate" (position 12, volume 390), "los barriles houses for sale" (position 15, volume 110), and "los barriles homes for sale" (position 16, volume 110). MexHome has /area/los-cabos/east-cape/los-barriles/ but no Los Barriles-specific blog content. A supporting blog post targeting this cluster (e.g., a buyer's guide or market overview for Los Barriles) would strengthen the existing area page and compete on the terms where Cabo Real Estate is actively gaining ground.

**4. Prioritize the fideicomiso content gap.**

MexHome already has /blog/bank-trusts-in-mexico/ and /blog/fideicomiso-ownership-mexican-real-estate/. Cabo Real Estate ranks at position 16 for "fideicomiso" (4,400 monthly searches) and has a dedicated explainer page that is also earning backlinks. MexHome should audit whether its existing fideicomiso content is well-optimized for this keyword and consider consolidating or strengthening the two existing posts into a single authoritative piece that can compete for this high-volume informational term.

**5. Address the structural weakness Cabo Real Estate exposes with its homepage-heavy strategy.**

The data shows Cabo Real Estate depending on its homepage to rank for dozens of high-commercial-intent Cabo and Los Cabos terms, and those rankings are broadly declining, likely because a homepage lacks the topical depth to hold positions as competition increases. MexHome's existing area pages for Los Cabos (/area/los-cabos/), Cabo San Lucas (/area/los-cabos/cabo-san-lucas/), and San Jose del Cabo (/area/los-cabos/san-jose-del-cabo/) are already structured correctly for this. Ensure these pages have substantive, regularly updated content so MexHome can capture traffic from buyers searching Cabo and San Jose del Cabo terms as Cabo Real Estate continues to lose ground on them.
