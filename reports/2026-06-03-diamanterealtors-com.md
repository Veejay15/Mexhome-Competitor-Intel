1. **Strengthen the Loreto page with a homes-for-sale sub-page.** Diamante Realtors just dropped from pos. 3 to pos. 7 for "homes for sale in loreto mexico" (480 searches/month). MexHome has /area/los-cabos/loreto/ but no homes-for-sale or condos-for-sale sub-pages beneath it. Building /area/los-cabos/loreto/loreto-homes-for-sale/ and /area/los-cabos/loreto/loreto-condos-for-sale/ now targets a gap Diamante is visibly losing ground in.

2. **Build Pescadero property-type sub-pages.** Diamante Realtors holds pos. 8-13 for a cluster of 590-volume Pescadero homes-for-sale terms. MexHome has /area/los-cabos/el-pescadero/ but no homes, condos, or land sub-pages beneath it. Add /area/los-cabos/el-pescadero/el-pescadero-homes-for-sale/ and /area/los-cabos/el-pescadero/el-pescadero-land-for-sale/ to compete directly. The keyword difficulty is low (7-10 per the data) and Diamante's positions are mid-page, not locked.

3. **Expand the East Cape content cluster.** Diamante Realtors has a dedicated /listings-real-estate-east-cape-any-price/ page that is their 10th most-linked page by referring domains (365 backlinks, cited in the summary data). MexHome has individual East Cape sub-area pages (Los Barriles, Cabo Pulmo, La Ribera, Zacatitos) but no consolidating East Cape hub page. Consider adding a /area/los-cabos/east-cape/ hub that aggregates those sub-areas and targets "east cape real estate" and "east cape baja real estate" cluster terms where Diamante holds mid-table positions.

4. **Expand the La Paz condos and homes pages with deeper content.** Diamante holds pos. 1 for "la paz condominiums" and "la ventana real estate," and their La Paz pages carry 158 referring domains (per the summary). MexHome already has /area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-condos-for-sale/ and /area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-homes-for-sale/. The action here is to update and expand those existing pages with more detailed content (pricing context, neighborhood guides, schema markup) rather than build new pages. Competing on these terms requires content depth, not just page existence.

5. **Do not pursue the backlink patterns Diamante is accumulating.** The bulk of their new links this week come from Nigerian news sites, wiki-spam domains, and gardening blogs. These are almost certainly spam placements and carry no strategic value. MexHome should focus link-building efforts on contextually relevant sources (travel, expat, relocation, real estate news) that Diamante's profile does not have.
