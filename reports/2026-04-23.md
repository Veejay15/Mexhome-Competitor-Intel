## 5. Recommended Actions

1. **Publish or strengthen a Puerto Vallarta homes-for-sale landing page.** Mexico Life has dropped from position 3 to unranked on "puerto vallarta homes for sale" (2,900/month). If MexHome has a comparable page, optimize it now with fresh content, updated listings, and internal links. If it does not have this page, build it this week. This is the single largest immediate keyword opportunity in this dataset.

2. **Build or upgrade a Nuevo Vallarta location page.** Mexico Life is winning "Things to know" SERP features for "nuevo vallarta" (12,100/month) and related queries. These features typically pull from pages with structured, factual destination content. MexHome needs a Nuevo Vallarta page that covers the neighborhood, lifestyle, and property types with enough depth to compete for these features.

3. **Pursue an AMPI Vallarta y Compostela listing.** The vallartanayaritmls.com link (ascore 55, do-follow) is Mexico Life's strongest backlink in this sample. Contact AMPI Vallarta directly to inquire about member directory inclusion. This is the highest-ROI link-building action available based on this week's data.

4. **Pitch MoneyDigest and similar retirement/expat publications.** Mexico Life has a live do-follow editorial mention in a MoneyDigest article about retiring in Mexico. Identify 5 to 10 similar articles ranking for "best places to retire in Mexico," "expat living Mexico," and "beach towns for retirees" and contact the authors or editors to suggest MexHome as a resource or to update existing content.

5. **Create or improve individual condo market pages for Bucerias, Punta Mita, and San Pancho.** Mexico Life is holding position 1 for "bucerias condos for sale" and position 1 for "punta mita condos for sale," both transactional keywords. These are low-competition (KD 0 and 0) with active buyer intent. If MexHome lacks dedicated pages for these locations, they can likely be ranked quickly given the low difficulty scores.

6. **Audit the Chacala and Guanajuato content gaps.** Mexico Life dropped from position 7 to unranked for "chacala mexico" (1,900/month) and slipped on "guanajuato city real estate" (320/month). These are geographic pages with thin competition. If MexHome has listings or agent coverage in these areas, publish location pages to capture the traffic Mexico Life is leaving behind.

7. **Monitor Mexico Life's /puerto-vallarta-real-estate.php for recovery.** This page is ranking for at least five keyword variants simultaneously and has dropped significantly on most of them this week. Watch whether this is a temporary algorithm fluctuation or a sustained decline. If rankings do not recover within two weeks, it signals a structural issue on their end and a longer window of opportunity for MexHome.
