## 5. Recommended Actions

1. **Audit MexHome's location-by-property-type page coverage this week.** Map the 20 markets Top Mexico Real Estate covers against MexHome's current URL structure. Identify which markets MexHome competes in and whether equivalent sub-pages exist (condos, oceanfront, pre-construction, retirement, land, homes). Prioritize pages for Puerto Vallarta, Riviera Nayarit, Tulum, and Cabo given search volume. Build missing pages if MexHome serves those markets.

2. **Build or strengthen a luxury sub-hub.** Top Mexico has `/luxury/[city]/` pages for 12+ markets. If MexHome does not have an equivalent luxury property vertical with city-level pages, create it. Start with the markets MexHome has the most listings in, and ensure each page targets "[city] luxury real estate" explicitly.

3. **Create a retirement buyer funnel.** Top Mexico has at least three distinct retirement-intent pages plus sub-pages for "retire in [city]" across all markets. This is a high-converting buyer segment. MexHome should have a dedicated retirement landing page and at least one "retire in [your top market]" page per served market.

4. **Capitalize on the VREG brand coverage.** Confirm that all VREG articles mentioning MexHome (Kinau Marina, Papalote Residences, Caivama, Naiara, Caza Mabo, Playa Cruz, Nayari, Main Street) include a link back to MexHome's site. If any are unlinked mentions, reach out to VREG to request a link. These are credible, niche-relevant backlinks in an industry where trust signals matter.

5. **Publish a "move-in ready properties" editorial page.** VREG is targeting this query cluster (e.g., "move-in ready properties Vallarta Nayarit 2026"). MexHome, as a platform with listings, is better positioned to rank for this query because it can back the editorial with an actual filtered listing page. Build the content and link it to a filtered search result showing immediately deliverable inventory.

6. **Create individual agent or advisor profile pages.** Top Mexico has built `/about-us/[agent-name]` pages. These drive branded search traffic, support E-E-A-T signals, and often rank for advisor name queries from referrals. If MexHome has a team or partner agents, create individual profile pages.

7. **Monitor Top Mexico's webinar pages for topic intelligence.** Their three active webinar pages cover: financing for foreigners, first-time buyer FAQs, and luxury homes in Riviera Maya. These topics reflect what their audience is actively asking. MexHome should consider whether blog posts or guides addressing these exact questions are live on the site, and create them if not.
