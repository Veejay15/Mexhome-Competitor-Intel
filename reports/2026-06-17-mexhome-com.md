This is a direct competitive signal that they are building a bookable tours or curated experiences product. MexHome (this platform) should evaluate whether a similar tours or guided property-viewing experience offering is feasible and, if so, move quickly before MexHome establishes brand recognition in that space.

2. **Strengthen the San Francisco Nayarit (San Pancho) landing page.** MexHome moved to position 1 on three keyword variants around "san francisco nayarit real estate" and "san pancho nayarit real estate" (590 volume each). MexHome already has the page at /area/riviera-nayarit-real-estate/san-francisco-mexico/ to compete. Expand that page with more structured content, current listing depth, and internal links from the San Pancho blog posts at /blog/real-estate-san-pancho-mexico/ and /blog/discover-san-pancho-a-hidden-gem-on-mexicos-riviera-nayarit-coast/ to reinforce the existing page rather than ceding this cluster.

3. **Reinforce the Los Cabos area pages.** MexHome gained position 1 for "los cabos real estate mexico" and is holding position 2 for "los cabos real estate" and "los cabos property" (all 590 volume). MexHome has strong coverage at /area/los-cabos/ and its sub-pages. Review internal linking from the existing Los Cabos blog content (including /blog/best-places-to-buy-real-estate-in-the-los-cabos-area/ and /blog/maximize-your-profit-los-cabos-investments-in-real-estate-for-2025/) to ensure authority flows to the main /area/los-cabos/ hub, competing directly with MexHome's ranked page.

4. **Target beachfront property keywords with a dedicated landing page.** MexHome's homepage jumped to position 1 for "mexico property for sale beachfront" (320 volume) and "beach property in mexico for sale" (320 volume), but these land on the generic homepage, not a purpose-built beachfront page. MexHome already has /area/mexico-vacation-rentals/mexico-beach-vacation-rentals/ but does not have a dedicated beachfront-property-for-sale page. A page structured around beachfront properties for sale across MexHome's core markets (Puerto Vallarta, Riviera Nayarit, Los Cabos) could outrank a homepage on this intent and capture more qualified buyers.

5. **Pursue the FlexMLS profile link opportunity.** MexHome gained a DA 88 link from FlexMLS (mymobile.flexmls.com). This is a verified real estate platform link. If MexHome does not already have a profile or verified listing on FlexMLS, this is a direct, high-authority link acquisition opportunity in a platform that lends legitimacy with real estate audiences.
