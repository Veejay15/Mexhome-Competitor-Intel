## 5. Recommended Actions for MexHome

The following recommendations are grounded in the competitive context of Baja properties as a Baja-focused platform, cross-referenced against MexHome's existing 2,153 content pages.

1. **Strengthen the Ensenada area hub.** MexHome has pages at `/area/ensenada/`, `/area/ensenada/ensenada-condos-for-sale/`, `/area/ensenada/ensenada-houses-for-sale/`, and `/area/ensenada/ensenada-land-for-sale/`. These pages exist but Baja properties holds domain authority in that market. Audit these pages for thin content, add current market context, and ensure internal linking from relevant blog posts flows into them.

2. **Strengthen the Rosarito area coverage.** MexHome has `/area/rosarito/` and `/area/rosarito/rosarito-condos-for-sale/` but lacks a Rosarito houses-for-sale page and a Rosarito land-for-sale page. Baja properties targets Rosarito directly. Building `/area/rosarito/rosarito-houses-for-sale/` and `/area/rosarito/rosarito-land-for-sale/` would complete the property-type set and close a gap that the competitor can exploit for long-tail search traffic.

3. **Expand Tijuana content depth.** MexHome has `/area/tijuana/`, `/area/tijuana/tijuana-condos-for-sale/`, `/area/tijuana/tijuana-houses-for-sale/`, and `/area/tijuana/tijuana-land-for-sale/`. Consider adding neighborhood-level sub-pages under Tijuana (similar to the depth MexHome has built under Puerto Vallarta and Los Cabos) to compete more aggressively with a Baja-native platform on Tijuana searches.

4. **Add a Baja California Norte area hub.** MexHome has `/area/baja-california-sur/` but no equivalent page for Baja California Norte. Baja properties covers both peninsulas. Creating `/area/baja-california-norte/` as a regional hub, with linking to Ensenada, Tijuana, and Rosarito sub-pages, would give MexHome a stronger topical signal for northern Baja searches where Baja properties has home-field advantage.

5. **Prioritize SEMrush data collection for Baja properties next cycle.** Two consecutive weeks without keyword and backlink data for this competitor means MexHome has no visibility into which Baja-focused terms Baja properties is ranking for or gaining on. Upload a SEMrush position and backlink export for bajaproperties.com before the next reporting cycle to enable concrete gap analysis.
