## 5. Recommended Actions for MexHome

1. **Strengthen the existing Los Cabos homes-for-sale pages against Baja properties' homepage dominance.** Baja properties holds positions 2 to 3 on a cluster of high-intent terms ("los cabos homes for sale," "homes for sale in los cabos," "cabo real estate for sale," "house in cabo") all pointing to their homepage. MexHome already has `/area/los-cabos/los-cabos-homes-for-sale/` and `/area/los-cabos/cabo-san-lucas/cabo-san-lucas-homes-for-sale/`. Audit internal linking to these pages and ensure on-page content depth (property counts, neighborhood breakdowns, schema markup) is competitive with what a homepage can deliver.

2. **Develop a Loreto real estate page.** Baja properties is gaining on "homes for sale in loreto baja mexico," "homes for sale loreto mexico," and "loreto homes for sale" with a dedicated URL at `/homes-for-sale-loreto-mexico/`. MexHome has `/area/los-cabos/loreto/` and `/area/los-cabos/loreto/nopolo/` and `/area/los-cabos/loreto/danzante-bay/`. These pages exist but should be expanded with explicit buy-intent content (homes for sale, condos for sale, land for sale sub-pages under Loreto) to compete directly on the transactional keyword variants Baja properties is climbing for.

3. **Target "cabo san lucas golf courses" and related golf content.** Baja properties holds position 1 on "cabo san lucas golf courses" (1,900 monthly searches) with a dedicated blog post, and this URL also attracts 96 referring domains, making it their second-strongest linked page after the homepage. MexHome has a blog post at `/blog/a-comprehensive-guide-to-puerto-vallartas-golf-courses/` covering Puerto Vallarta golf but no equivalent for Los Cabos. Building a dedicated Cabo golf guide would target a keyword where Baja properties is entrenched and earning links.

4. **Publish a hacienda homes content piece.** "Hacienda for sale" jumped 41 positions for Baja properties this week via their blog post. MexHome has no equivalent content in the existing page list. A blog post targeting "hacienda for sale Mexico," "Mexican hacienda house," and related terms represents an uncontested content gap, particularly given Baja properties is now ranked 21st and climbing on a 590-volume keyword with low difficulty (6).

5. **Monitor and counter the Loreto content cluster.** Three Loreto keyword variants moved up simultaneously for Baja properties this week, signaling coordinated content or link activity on their `/homes-for-sale-loreto-mexico/` page. MexHome should add transactional sub-pages under `/area/los-cabos/loreto/` (e.g., Loreto homes for sale, Loreto condos for sale, Loreto land for sale) before Baja properties consolidates page-one positions on these terms. MexHome already has the parent Loreto area page; adding property-type children is the fastest path to competing.

6. **Do not attempt to compete on "cabo san lucas" (135,000 searches, difficulty 76) with a single page.** Baja properties sits at position 11 on this term with a blog post, not a transactional page. The keyword difficulty and SERP feature density (knowledge graph, video, Twitter) make this a long-term brand play, not a quick win. MexHome's effort is better directed at the transactional Cabo sub-terms (positions 2 to 5, difficulty under 20) where Baja properties is vulnerable and MexHome has existing area pages to strengthen.
