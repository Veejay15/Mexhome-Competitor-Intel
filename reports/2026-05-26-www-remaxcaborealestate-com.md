## 5. Recommended Actions for MexHome

**1. Strengthen the San Jose del Cabo properties pages, not build new ones.**
MexHome already has /area/los-cabos/san-jose-del-cabo/, /area/los-cabos/san-jose-del-cabo/san-jose-del-cabo-condos-for-sale/, and /area/los-cabos/san-jose-del-cabo/san-jose-del-cabo-homes-for-sale/. Remax is surging on SJdC terms ("san jose del cabo real estate" from position 15 to 4, "homes for sale san jose del cabo" from 18 to 5) but doing so entirely through their homepage, not a dedicated page. MexHome's dedicated SJdC pages are a structural advantage. Audit and strengthen these pages with current listing counts, neighborhood context, and on-page signals to widen the gap before Remax builds out proper sub-pages.

**2. Target East Cape more aggressively.**
Remax jumped from position 11 to position 2 for "real estate east cape baja" (volume: 260) using their /east-cape/properties page. MexHome has /area/los-cabos/east-cape/ and several sub-pages (Los Barriles, Cabo Pulmo, Zacatitos, etc.). Review whether these pages are internally linked effectively, have sufficient on-page content, and are targeting the exact phrase "east cape baja real estate" or "real estate east cape baja" directly in headings and meta titles.

**3. Target Loreto real estate keywords.**
Remax holds position 5 for "loreto homes for sale" (volume: 480) via a dedicated /loreto/properties page. MexHome has /area/los-cabos/loreto/ and /area/los-cabos/loreto/nopolo/ and /area/los-cabos/loreto/danzante-bay/. Check whether the Loreto parent page is explicitly targeting "loreto homes for sale" and "loreto real estate" in its title tag and H1. These are achievable terms at low keyword difficulty (13) where MexHome's existing page structure should compete.

**4. Target "cabos real estate" and "cabo houses for sale" variant terms via the existing Los Cabos pages.**
Remax ranks position 1 for "cabos real estate" (volume: 320, difficulty: 17) and position 1 for "cabo houses" and "house in cabo" (volume: 480 each). MexHome has /area/los-cabos/cabo-san-lucas/cabo-san-lucas-homes-for-sale/ and /area/los-cabos/los-cabos-homes-for-sale/. Ensure these pages are targeting casual search phrasing ("cabo houses," "houses in cabo") in addition to formal phrasing. Difficulty scores in the 10 to 23 range mean these are within reach with minimal content investment.

**5. Do not pursue Remax's link-building approach. Pursue content-led link equity instead.**
Remax's backlink profile (224 links, 111 domains, domain inlink rank 34) is concentrated on three pages and heavy in branded anchor text. A significant share of their anchor distribution appears in spam-adjacent patterns (the "LINKS FOR ... TELEGRAM @happygrannypies" anchor appearing across 15 referring domains is a red flag). MexHome should focus on earning contextually relevant links to its area pages and blog content from US-based real estate media, expat communities, and travel publications, where Remax has no meaningful presence.

**6. Monitor San Jose del Cabo and East Cape pages closely next cycle.**
Given Remax's sharp upward movement on SJdC terms this period (multiple keywords jumping 7 to 13 positions simultaneously), there may be a technical update, content refresh, or link addition to those pages that is not yet visible in this dataset. These are competitive terms where MexHome has existing assets and should not cede ground.
