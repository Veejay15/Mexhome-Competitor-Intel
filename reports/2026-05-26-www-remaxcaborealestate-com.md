Audit both pages for content depth, internal linking, and schema. Add localized neighborhood detail and recent market context to differentiate from Remax's homepage-based rankings on these terms.

2. **Reinforce the East Cape content cluster before Remax consolidates.** Remax jumped from position 11 to 2 on "real estate east cape baja" via a dedicated /east-cape/properties page. MexHome has /area/los-cabos/east-cape/ plus sub-pages for Cabo Pulmo, Los Barriles, La Ribera, Zacatitos, and others. Expand the parent /east-cape/ page with a proper editorial introduction covering East Cape's distinct value proposition (off-grid, surf, marine reserve proximity). 3. **Target MLS-intent keywords.** Remax moved from position 11 to 2 on "cabo san lucas mls listing" and from 12 to 3 on "cabo san lucas mls listings" (170 monthly searches each). MexHome does not appear to have a page explicitly targeting MLS search intent for Cabo. Consider adding a dedicated blog post or landing page addressing how the Los Cabos MLS works for buyers, linking to the /area/los-cabos/ property search. This is a genuine content gap.

4. **Monitor internal cannibalization as a defensive signal.** Remax shows "san jose del cabo homes for sale" ranking on two separate pages simultaneously (homepage and /san-jose-del-cabo/properties), with the dedicated page declining. Ensure MexHome's own SJdC pages are not similarly splitting signals. Consolidate anchor text in internal links to point clearly to the dedicated sub-pages rather than to the Los Cabos parent page.

5. **Watch Remax's backlink toxic anchor cluster.** The "LINKS FOR www.remaxcaborealestate.com TELEGRAM @happygrannypies" anchor appears across 15 referring domains. This is a pattern consistent with a spam link scheme. If this leads to a manual action or algorithmic penalty against Remax, MexHome should be positioned to capture the traffic displacement quickly, particularly on the core "los cabos real estate" and "cabo san lucas homes for sale" keywords where Remax currently holds position 1.
