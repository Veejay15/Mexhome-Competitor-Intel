## 5. Recommended Actions for MexHome

**1. Target the Cancun homes cluster where TopMexicoRealEstate is strongest.**
Their positions 1-3 on "cancun homes for sale," "cancun real estate," "cancun mexico real estate," and related variants are built on a legacy .php URL structure (cancun-real-estate/homes-cancun.php). MexHome already has `/area/cancun/cancun-houses-for-sale/` and `/area/cancun/cancun-condos-for-sale/`. Strengthen both pages with expanded content: neighborhood breakdowns, price range guidance, and buyer-intent copy. The competitor's .php architecture is technically less favorable for modern indexing. MexHome's clean URL structure is an advantage to press.

**2. Exploit the "merida mexico real estate" ranking decline.**
TopMexicoRealEstate dropped from position 10 to 19 on "merida mexico real estate" (2,400 sv). MexHome already has `/area/merida/`, `/area/merida/merida-condos-for-sale/`, `/area/merida/merida-houses-for-sale/`, and `/area/merida/merida-land-for-sale/`. This is a direct window to capture traffic from a weakening competitor. Audit those existing pages for content depth and add the elements that are likely missing: neighborhood guides, cost-of-living context, expat community information, and internal links from relevant blog content.

**3. Press the "la paz mexico real estate" confusion on TopMexicoRealEstate's side.**
They are cannibalizing themselves on this term across two URLs, with one dropping from 4 to 19 and the other jumping from 34 to 7. The instability signals an unresolved internal competition between their pages. MexHome has `/area/los-cabos/la-paz/`, `/area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-condos-for-sale/`, `/area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-homes-for-sale/`, and `/area/los-cabos/la-paz/la-paz-land-for-sale/`. Strengthen these pages with consolidated, authoritative content targeting "la paz mexico real estate" and "houses for sale in la paz mexico" (590 sv, where TopMexicoRealEstate still holds position 5 but is slipping from position 2). A well-optimized MexHome La Paz homes page has a realistic path to that top-5 slot.

**4. Build content authority around Rocky Point (Puerto Penasco) to challenge TopMexicoRealEstate's dominant position 1 hold.**
TopMexicoRealEstate holds position 1 for "homes for sale in rocky point mexico" (880 sv) and "houses for sale in rocky point mx" (480 sv). MexHome has `/area/puerto-penasco/` and several neighborhood sub-pages (cholla-bay, las-conchas, sandy-beach, etc.) but no dedicated homes-for-sale or condos-for-sale pages under that hub. There is a genuine content gap here. Consider adding `/area/puerto-penasco/puerto-penasco-homes-for-sale/` and `/area/puerto-penasco/puerto-penasco-condos-for-sale/` as structured listing pages targeting these transactional keywords directly.

**5. Monitor and flag TopMexicoRealEstate's panel subdomain situation.**
Their panel.topmexicorealestate.com/free-minds/ subdirectory is producing indexed content on completely off-topic subjects. If Google's quality algorithms treat subdomain content as part of the root domain's topical profile (which is inconsistent but possible), this is diluting their authority. MexHome has no action to take here other than consistent topical focus in its own publishing, which works to its advantage over time as TopMexicoRealEstate accumulates off-topic indexed pages.

**6. Do not pursue link acquisition from the types of sources appearing in TopMexicoRealEstate's backlink data.**
Every meaningful new link in their top 25 rows this period comes from spam networks, aged-domain link sellers, or dormant legacy blogs. There is no signal here of a deliberate, quality link-building campaign from TopMexicoRealEstate. MexHome should maintain its focus on editorial and contextual link acquisition and not be distracted by volume metrics on this competitor's backlink count.
